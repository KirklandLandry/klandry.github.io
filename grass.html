Interactive Grass Rendering

An interactive grass renderer using compute shaders that can handle hundreds of thousands of grass blades while maintaining a high frame rate.

Details

An interactive grass system I created featuring:

  • Support for all light types
  • Affectors that can bend grass blades near them in a variety of different patterns/motions
  • Unlimited grass affectors
  • Directional scrolling wind noise
  • Blade cutting
  • Frustum culling
  • Colour painting
  • Supports any mesh type for rendering (with textures), not just a fixed grass blade mesh

Some video examples showing support for all light types, leaving trails behind (the time grass stays down can be modified), and painting grass blade colours

Along with just pushing grass outwards, grass collision effectors can also produce a variety of patterns through mixing different parameters.
